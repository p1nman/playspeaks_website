Fees & Policies

Treatment session fees and practice policies

Session Fees

  • Initial Intake session (50 minutes): $205
  • Individual (50 minutes) or Play Therapy (45 minutes) session: $180
  • Family session (50 minutes): $180
  • Parenting session (50 minutes): $180
  • Hourly rate for Collaborative Divorce cases: $180

Payment & Insurance: Celeste Inman currently does not accept insurance or third-party payers. Payment is expected at the time services are rendered. If you would like a receipt (superbill) coded for insurance so you may submit it to your insurance provider, please request it at the time of service.

Sliding scale: A limited number of sliding-scale slots are available on a case-by-case basis. If needed, please inquire.

Scheduling & cancellations: Services are by appointment only. A 24-hour cancellation policy is in effect; if an appointment is not canceled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time, the full fee will be charged for the missed service.

Insurance & Superbills

Celeste Inman is considered an out-of-network provider. If desired, a superbill with appropriate CPT and diagnostic codes can be provided so you may submit for potential reimbursement from your insurer.

The “No Surprises Act” & Good Faith Estimate

Good Faith Estimate: Under the No Surprises Act (Public Health Service Act Section 2799B-6), clients who are uninsured or choose not to use insurance have the right to receive a written Good Faith Estimate of expected charges for therapy services. You may request a Good Faith Estimate at any time.

If you receive a bill that is $400 or more than your Good Faith Estimate, you have the right to dispute the charge. For more information about your rights and how to obtain a Good Faith Estimate, visit www.cms.gov/nosurprises or call HHS at 1-800-958-3059.
